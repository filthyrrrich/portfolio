About Me

Richard Schiffer Jr. is an American born poker player and skateboarder currently living in Colorado.


Richard (Rich or Filthy Rich) was born in Silicon Valley, California; I guess you could say technology is in his blood! He lived in the south bay area for 20 years before moving out to the Rocky Mountains. In Colorado, he met his beautiful wife, Alyssa, and together they discovered their passion for playing poker. His background for most of his working life has been in sales and customer service. Recently, he has been working in the casino industry as a Table Games Dealer (BlackJack, Roulette, Poker), but is ready to start the next chapter of his life in the tech industry.


Rich is a newly graduated Full-Stack Web Developer from the University of Denver and his wife is currently pursuing her nursing degree at the University of Colorado. Upon graduation he started his own company (Freeroll, LLC). He is currently working on various freelance projects while searching for a full-time job.
