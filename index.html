About

"I never lose. I either win or learn."
- Nelson Mandela

A creator with a passion for functionality and design.

Born and raised in Silicon Valley, you could say technology is in my blood. I spent my early years playing music and skateboarding through the streets of the south bay area. Being a fan of the outdoors, I moved to Colorado in 2008 and met my beautiful wife, Alyssa, shortly there after. My background for most of my working life has been in sales, customer service, and hospitality.

I graduated from the University of Denver in 2018 as a full stack web developer. Upon graduation, I started my own company (Freeroll, LLC) and am currently working on various freelance projects while searching for a full-time job.
